Soyaki Salmon on Rice

Description

Looking for a healthy and filling meal? Look no further! Soyaki salmon on rice to the rescue!

With only a few ingredients (all of which are available at your local Trader Joe's), You can start munching on salmon and rice in no time. Check out the ingredients and recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of salmon
  • 1 cup of Trader Joe's Soyaki sauce
  • 1 cup white rice
  • A pinch of Everything but the Bagel seasoning
  • A vegetable side as seen in the picture is optional. I'm usually full enough without a side.
  • A rice cooker. I won't help you if you don't have one.

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Put your salmon in an oven-safe container and pour one cup of Soyaki sauce over it.
  3. Wash your rice, then put into a rice cooker (I don't give instructions for those who don't have rice cookers).
  4. Place salmon in the oven for 25 minutes.
  5. Once rice and salmon are finished cooking, place rice in a bowl and put salmon on top.
  6. Sprinkle with Everything but the Bagel seasoning for plating, and...
  7. Dig in!
