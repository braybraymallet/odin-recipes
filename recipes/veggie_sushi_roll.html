Veggie Sushi Rolls

Description

Believe it or not, this meal is for you. You, too, can make sushi at home! Veggie sushi rolls are now easier than ever with just about any basic at-home sushi kit from Amazon!

With healthy vegetables, you can have a full plate of sushi rolls in 30 minutes or less without half the expense of going out to eat. Follow the recipe below to get started.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice (this can be found at any Asian mart)
  • A drizzle of rice vinegar
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 sheets of nori
  • Spicy mayo for plating
  • A pinch of sesame seeds
  • 2 cups of white vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar

Directions

  1. Combine white vinegar, water, and sugar into a pot, then bring to a boil.
  2. Peel carrot into thin strips. Once finished, shove carrots into a mason jar. After the water has come to a boil, pour into the mason jar with the carrots and let it sit until it cools down to room temperature. This will give you pickled carrots!
  3. Clean rice and add to rice cooker (if you don't have a rice cooker, find another recipe because you shouldn't be making this).
  4. Cut cucumber into batons. The batons should be as wide as the nori.
  5. Cut avocado into strips.
  6. Check on your rice. If it is ready, spread it out on a baking sheet, cover with rice vinegar, stir to coat, then place in the freezer until cool.
  7. Drain your carrots once they have cooled down.
  8. Once your rice is cool and your avocado, carrot, and cucumber is prepared, bring them to a staging area.
  9. From there, line your sushi bazooka (again, get this before you start this recipe - sorry if you're reading this too late. Your fault) with rice. You will need to wet your hands. I recommend having a spare bowl with water in the staging area. Create a dimple in the rice to place fillings.
  10. Add the appropriate amount of cucumber, carrot, and avocado for your liking. Close the sushi bazooka, compress the sushi, and push it out onto a sheet of nori.
  11. Roll the sushi in the nori, cut into thin strips, then drizzle with spicy mayo and sesame seeds.
  12. Repeat until all the ingredients are gone.
  13. You should now have at least 2 homemade veggie sushi rolls! Tanoshimu.
