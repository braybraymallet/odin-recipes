Protein Balls

Description

Craving a tasty snack but don't want to worry about calories, extended recipes, or egregious baking times? Protein power balls are the choice for you!

With simple ingredients (many of which can be found in the pantry already), you can turn these healthy snacks around in 30 minutes or less. Follow the recipe below for bite-sized, lip-smacking snacks in half an hour's time!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (or other mix-ins)
  • 1 tbs flax seed
  • 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine peanut butter, honey, and vanilla. Stir to combine.
  2. Add oats, coconut, flaxseed, sea salt, and cinnamon and stir until combined.
  3. Add mini chocolate chips (or preferred mix-ins) and stir until evenly distributed.
  4. Use a 1 TBS measuring spoon to portion out the mixture and roll it into balls.
  5. Put the balls in an airtight container and chill for 30-60 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
